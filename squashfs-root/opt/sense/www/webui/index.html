Browsing protection helps you evaluate the safety of web sites you visit, prevents you from unintentionally accessing harmful web sites and blocks connections to servers that are known to be end-points for malware.

Tracking protection allows you to block sites from sharing your personal information, like browsing habits, to a third party. Enable tracking protection if you want added privacy and security when browsing the web.
